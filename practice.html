How to Make a Sandwich

  1. Get required supplies (bread, peanut butter, jelly and knife, plate).
  2. Open loaf of bread and grab two slices.
  3. Place the slices next to each other on the plate
  4. Open the peanut butter and use the knife to spread desired amount on one of the slices of bread.
  5. Open the jelly and use the knife to spread desired amount of jelly on the same slice of bread.
  6. Grab the slice without the toppings and place on top so that the corresponding crust lines up.
  7. Clean off utensils and return the supplies to their proper place.
  8. Enjoy!
